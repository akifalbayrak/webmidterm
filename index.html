Welcome to Hamsi Bank

What is our vision

Hamsi Bank is a company that offers HamsiCoin (HMS), Turkey's first and only anchovy-based cryptocurrency. HamsiCoin is a safe, fast and environmentally friendly cryptocurrency based on the popularity and value of anchovy fish. HamsiCoin is designed to promote the conservation of anchovy fish stocks and sustainable fishing. HamsiCoin is distributed as a reward to individuals and organizations that hunt or consume anchovy fish. HamsiCoin can also be used to buy and sell products and services related to anchovy fish.

Hamsi Bank offers a number of services to support HamsiCoin. These include Hamsi Wallet, Hamsi Exchange, Hamsi Pay, Hamsi Card and Hamsi Charity. Hamsi Wallet is a digital wallet where users can store and manage their HamsiCoins. Hamsi Exchange is a platform where users can exchange HamsiCoins for other cryptocurrencies or fiat currencies. Hamsi Pay is a payment system where users can shop online or offline with their HamsiCoins. Hamsi Card is a physical card with which users can withdraw their HamsiCoins from ATMs or spend them from POS devices. Hamsi Charity is a donation platform where users can donate their HamsiCoins to charities or social projects.

Hamsi Bank has undertaken a mission to spread the love and culture of anchovy fish. Hamsi Bank supports education, research, art, sports and entertainment activities related to anchovy fish. Hamsi Bank also organizes various campaigns and events to raise awareness about anchovy and ensure community participation. Hamsi Bank aims to create a community where anchovy fish enthusiasts come together, share and learn.

Hamsi Bank is an innovative and visionary company offering HamsiCoin, a cryptocurrency based on anchovy fish. Hamsi Bank works to facilitate and enrich everything related to anchovy fish. Hamsi Bank offers an opportunity to carry the love and culture of anchovy fish into the future. Hamsi Bank invites everyone who loves anchovy to HamsiCoin.
